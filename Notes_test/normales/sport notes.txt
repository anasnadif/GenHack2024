Basics of Boxing:

Stance:

Orthodox vs. Southpaw.
Balanced weight distribution, knees slightly bent.
Jab:

Fast, straight punch with the lead hand.
Set up combinations and gauge distance.
Cross:

Powerful punch with the rear hand.
Rotate hips and shoulders for maximum impact.
Defense:

Slip, bob and weave to avoid punches.
Practice blocking and parrying.
